The stack

Four systems, one read-out.

Each layer works standalone or together — hardware in the water, software on land.

◉

Water Sensors

DO, pH, salinity, ammonia and temperature logged every 90 seconds from floating or submerged buoys.

◈

AI Vision

Underwater cameras track biomass, feeding response and early signs of sea lice or disease.

⟐

Auto-Feed Control

Feeders adjust pellet volume in real time based on appetite signals, cutting overfeeding.

⌁

Farmer App

One app per site — alerts, trends and harvest planning, usable from a boat with patchy signal.

How it connects

From the water to your pocket in four steps.

01

Sense

Buoys and probes sample water and fish activity continuously.

02

Transmit

LoRa mesh network relays readings to a shoreline gateway, no SIM needed.

03

Analyze

Models flag anomalies against each site's own historical baseline.

04

Act

Farmer gets a plain-language alert, with a suggested action, in seconds.
